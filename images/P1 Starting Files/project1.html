About Us

About Us

Find out more about our department

The office of the Director of the Hayden Planetarium operates out of the Department of Astrophysics at the American Museum of Natural History. Our mission is to bring the frontier of astrophysics to the public via exhibitry, books, public programs, and on-line resources.

Niel Degrass Tyson and Apollo 17 Commander Gene Cernman drive a replica of the Lunar Rover on the Ross Terrence of the Rose Center of Earth and Space.

The Department of Education at the American Museum of Natural History continues the mission with programs for school children, families and teachers.
